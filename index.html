Standards & Compliance

This field excursion risk assessment form complies with:

  • AS/NZS ISO 31000:2018 - Risk management guidelines
  • AS/NZS 4801:2001 - Occupational health and safety management
  • Education Department Excursion Guidelines - Field trip requirements
  • Work Health and Safety Act 2011 - Duty of care obligations

For conservation field work: Always conduct a site reconnaissance, check weather forecasts, ensure appropriate PPE, and have emergency communication plans.
