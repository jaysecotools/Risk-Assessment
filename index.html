Risk Assessment Definitions

Likelihood Definitions:
  • Rare: May occur only in exceptional circumstances (<1% chance)
  • Unlikely: Could occur but not expected (1-10% chance)
  • Possible: Might occur at some time (10-50% chance)
  • Likely: Will probably occur (50-90% chance)
  • Almost Certain: Expected to occur (>90% chance)
Severity Definitions:
  • Insignificant: No injury, negligible financial loss
  • Minor: First aid injury, minor property damage
  • Moderate: Medical treatment, moderate property damage
  • Major: Serious injury, major property damage
  • Catastrophic: Fatality, permanent disability, major environmental damage
